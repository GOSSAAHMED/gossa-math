Chapter 18 — Hierarchical Series (A Substitute for Taylor)

Approximating functions via relative structure

18.1 Why Taylor Is Not Enough

Taylor expansion is local and often diverges for exponential or super-exponential growth. Hierarchical series preserve structure instead.

18.2 Core Idea

Instead of expanding in \((x-x_0)\), hierarchical series expand in \(\ln(x/x_0)\) or \(x/x_0\).

18.3 First-Level Relative Expansion

\[ f(x)\approx f(x_0)\exp\!\left( D_1f(x_0)\ln\frac{x}{x_0} \right) \]
