Hierarchical Calculus

A mathematical framework for structured and relative change

This book presents Hierarchical Calculus as a unified mathematical framework that extends classical differential calculus by introducing hierarchical levels of change. The theory is developed rigorously from first principles and progressively connected to modern scientific modeling.
